Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to showcase various gadgets from Splatoon. It would be like a fake online shopping site pretending to be in the universe of Splatoon.

Audience

The audience for this website would be fans of the Splatoon video game series who are interested in the various gadgets and gear featured in the game.

Dynamic elements

The javascript on this site will provide interactive elements such as image sliders to showcase different gadgets, and possibly a shopping cart feature to simulate purchasing items.
